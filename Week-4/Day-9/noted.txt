Main Components of Transformers
A. Embedding Layer

Words are converted into vectors.

Example:

"cat" → [0.25, 0.81, 0.13]

This numerical representation helps neural networks understand language.

B. Positional Encoding

Transformers process words in parallel.

So:

they need position information.

Example:

"I eat rice"

is different from:

"Rice eat I"

Positional encoding tells:

word order.
C. Self-Attention

This is the heart of Transformers.

Self-attention helps determine:

which words are important.
Example

Sentence:

"The boy kicked the ball because he was angry."

Transformer learns:

"he" refers to "boy"

using attention.

6. What is Attention Mechanism?

Attention allows model to:

focus on relevant words.
Human Analogy

When reading:

humans focus on important words.

Transformers do the same.
